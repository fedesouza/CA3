Candle magic

Candle Magic has been around for many, many years, being traced as far back as the Palaeolithic era. It is a very powerful form of sympathetic magic.

Magic has consequences, both positive and negative, and its effects ripple through the world. Be cautious with your intentions and choose your words carefully.

For effective candle magic, consider the following:

Precision: Be clear and concise in your wording, covering all bases.

Repetition: Some candle magic requires multiple days; choose an undisturbed space.

Safety: Avoid fire hazards, keep candles away from curtains, and choose a low-traffic area.

By understanding these basics, you can perform candle magic with intention and care.
